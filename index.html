About Our Mission

Gokulites for Kidney Care is a dedicated initiative led by students of Sree Gokulam Public School, aiming to raise funds for a local kidney patient in urgent need of a transplant. Our goal is to bring hope, healing, and support through collective efforts.

How You Can Help

  • Donate directly (bank/UPI details shared in school).
  • Share our campaign with friends and family.
  • Volunteer to organize events or spread awareness.
